
# The Backwoods

> *A hostile dimension where wooden creatures called Splinters hunt you down — but only when you're not looking.*

## About

The Backwoods adds a dark new dimension to Minecraft. Built from oak and shadow, it's a foggy, cave-less world frozen at permanent midnight where ash drifts through the air.

Inside, you'll find **Splinters**, hostile, humanoid creatures made of oak plank that relentlessly pursue you. They mine through walls, build bridges, and climb to reach you. But stare them down and they freeze completely. Look away for even a second and they're moving again.

Stay vigilant. In The Backwoods, looking away is how you die.

---

## Features

- **The Backwoods Dimension** dense fog, permanent midnight, no caves, ash particle effects
- **Wood Plains Biome**  oak plank terrain with oak log underground layers
- **Splinter Mob**  stare-to-freeze mechanic, mines through blocks, builds bridges and climbs to reach the player
- **Flint and Charcoal**  a new crafting item used to light the portal

---

## How to Enter

1. Build a portal frame out of **oak planks** (same shape as a Nether portal)
2. Craft **Flint and Charcoal** using 1 flint + 1 charcoal (same recipe layout as Flint and Steel)
3. Light the portal frame with your Flint and Charcoal
4. Step through

---

## The Splinter

Splinters are the sole inhabitants of The Backwoods. They are relentless and intelligent.

- **Stare-to-freeze**  if you look directly at a Splinter, it cannot move
- **Block mining**  when you're not looking, Splinters mine through walls to reach you
- **Building**  Splinters will place oak planks to climb up and bridge toward you
- **30 HP**  they hit hard and don't give up
- **Drops** an oak plank on death
